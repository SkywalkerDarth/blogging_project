Reservoir Engineering

Reservoir simulation is an important part of Petroleum Engineering. It involves a complex process for Reservoir Engineers this involves the input of static models gotten from production seismologist, production geology, and petrophysicist, then Production data(History), pvt modelling(Fluid Data), Relative Permeability modelling(Rock Data), are imputed to initialize the model, well data is added and history matching is made to validate model input and then it is used for prediction based on various development strategies.
