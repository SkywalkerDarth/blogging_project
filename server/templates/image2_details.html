Carnot Cycle

Carnot Cycle is the maximum possible efficiency of a heat engine operating between two reservoirs at different temperatures. The higher called the heat source and the lower the heat sink. It was proposed by Sadi Carnot. It works in a Reversible isothermal and adiabatic Expansion, then Reversible isothermal and adiabatic Compression, which forms a cycle at the end.
